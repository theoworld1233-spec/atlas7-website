WORKFLOW

The Blueprint Loop

We align extensively on your core spatial goals, budgetary parameters, and site contextual limitations in Adama or surrounding communities. Our discovery phase guarantees we lay down the optimal strategic vision before drawing a single technical layout.
We translate abstract programmatic requirements into physical volumetric massing sketches. This phase establishes the core geometric logic, interlocking cantilevers, solar paths, and floor alignments that dictate the future building.
We refine our initial concepts into high-fidelity models. We meticulously study materials (raw concrete, local masonry, glass), interior spatial voids, and custom lighting parameters. You see photo-realistic visual renders of your building's form.
Our design transforms into engineering codes. We produce hyper-detailed mechanical, plumbing, electrical, and structural sets, material spec sheets, and foundation layout maps. Contractors get absolute blueprint clarity.
Tewodros personally oversees critical phases at the construction site. We supervise material casting, alignment tolerances, structural execution, and interior finishes to guarantee the physical reality is as perfect as the initial vision.
